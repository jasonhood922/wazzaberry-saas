# WazzaBerry Brand Guidelines

## Essence
WazzaBerry is a radar for buyers. The brand voice is confident, plain-spoken, and a little playful — it talks about outcomes (meetings, pipeline) rather than features, and never uses growth-hacker jargon.

**Tagline:** Your AI sales agent for warm, ready-to-buy leads.
**Elevator pitch:** Drop in your website URL. WazzaBerry learns what you sell, hunts down your highest-intent prospects, and starts personalised conversations across email and social — automatically.

## Logo
- Primary mark: a berry built from dots with a radar arc — "a cluster of signals."
- Canonical vector: `components/Logo.tsx` (SVG). Raster: `public/brand/logo.png`. Favicon: `app/icon.svg`.
- Keep clear space around the mark equal to one dot diameter. Don't recolour, rotate, or add effects.

Defined as Tailwind v4 `@theme` tokens in `app/globals.css`.

## Typography
- Geist Sans for everything; Geist Mono for numbers, step counters, and stat values.
- Headlines: extrabold, tight tracking. One gradient-highlighted phrase per hero maximum.

## Voice rules
1. Lead with the outcome ("Meetings land in your calendar"), not the mechanism.
2. Short sentences. No exclamation marks in body copy.
3. The agent is "your agent" — never "our AI" or "the bot."
4. Be honest: label demo data, AI-generated imagery, and illustrative numbers as such.

## Imagery
- Product-first: dashboard mockups and UI over stock photography.
- Portraits: AI-generated placeholders are currently used for testimonial figures and are labelled as illustrative; swap in real customers as they arrive.
